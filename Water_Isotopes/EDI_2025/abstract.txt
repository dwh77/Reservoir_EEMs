Samples of stable water isotopes (delta 18O and delta 2H) were collected from surface waters and depth profiles in Carvins Cove Reservoir (Ronaoke, Virginia, USA) a drinking water reservoir owned and operated by the Western Virginia Water Authority as a primary drinking water sources for Roanoke, Virginia. Samples were collected approximately monthly at two sites along a primary tributary and depth profiles at multiple transects within Carvins Cove Reservoir from May 2024 - April 2025. Samples were analyzed using cavity ringdown spectroscopy and reported as deviation of concentration from that of Vienna standard mean ocean water.
